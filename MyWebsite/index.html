Guatemala is primarily known for its extensive Mayan ruins, particularly the Tikal National Park, considered the heartland of the ancient Mayan civilization, as well as its impressive volcanic landscapes with over 30 volcanoes, including active ones like Fuego Volcano, making it a popular destination for volcano tours and hikes; it is also considered the birthplace of chocolate, with the Mayans being the first to cultivate cacao beans and create chocolate drinks.

Recommended places to visit in Guatemala

  • Antigua Guatemala
  • The Mayan Ruins of Tikal
  • Lake Atitlan
  • Chichicastenango
  • Semuc Champey
  • Acatenango and Fuego Volcanos
